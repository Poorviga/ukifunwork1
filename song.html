Velaikkaran is a 2017 Tamil language action film written and directed by Mohan Raja. The film was produced by RD Raja. The film also stars Shiva Karthikeyan, Bhagat Basil and Nayanthara. Music by Anirudh Ravichandran and cinematography by Ramji. The movie, which cost Rs 40 crore, was released on December 22, 2017
